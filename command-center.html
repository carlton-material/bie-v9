Methodology
Glass Box · How This Brief Was Generated ▾

Data Sources: This morning brief synthesizes 862 live signals across three layers — 318 human-expressive (survey responses, interviews, review sentiment), 295 behavioral (booking patterns, abandonment rates, session analytics), and 249 cultural (market trends, regulatory filings, social listening).


Composite Methodology: The Brand Score Index (72) is a weighted composite of 6 dimensions mapped to M+'s proprietary framework. Scores are normalized to a 0-100 scale with confidence intervals derived from signal volume and recency. Delta values compare to the prior 30-day wave.


Priority Ranking: Alerts are ranked by a severity score combining signal convergence (multiple layers confirming the same pattern), velocity (rate of change), and business impact (estimated revenue at risk). HIGH = 3+ layer convergence + accelerating velocity.


Pattern Detection: Active patterns are identified through unsupervised clustering of signal embeddings. A pattern is surfaced when 3+ signals from different sources converge within a 7-day window with >0.7 cosine similarity.

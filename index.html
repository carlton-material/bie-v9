The Shift

The Shift: Decision Engine

The difference between a quarterly earnings report and a Bloomberg Terminal. One tells you what happened. The other integrates live feeds, models scenarios, and surfaces the signal that matters now.
What Changed Traditional Tracker AI-Native Intelligence
What changed? Periodic measurement → Continuous sensing
Why did it change? One primary data source → Multiple imperfect signals
Who experienced it? Backward-looking → Forward-looking & predictive
What if we do X vs Y? Fixed constructs → Evolving constructs
Why Now

In a world where products and services can be created overnight, brand is the one thing that still compounds. AI agents are increasingly mediating how consumers discover, compare, and choose — and they don't care about your tagline. They care about signal: sentiment, utility, trust. The brands that understand this shift will own the next decade. The ones that don't will be disintermediated by the platforms and agents sitting between them and their customers.

The Story

Stayworthy is losing ground where it matters most: Dependable (58, −6) and Personal (64, −8) are in structural decline. Customers still find the brand accessible and top-of-mind, but the experience isn't keeping its promises. This is an operational problem showing up as a brand problem — and at current trajectory, Stayworthy drops below competitive median in two quarters.

Signal Types

Signal Types: Going Deeper

Layer 01
Human-Expressive
The hardest to explain and the most defensible. Plays to what Material+ already does well—qual-at-scale.
The AI-native version runs asynchronous moderated interviews—a 10-minute conversation where the AI adapts follow-ups based on what someone actually says.
What We Already Do
Qual at scale Quant surveys IDIs Focus groups Ethnography CX measurement
Rationale
Traditional tracking is taking someone's temperature. This is reading their bloodwork.
Layer 02
Behavioral & Market
The reality-check layer. Connecting perception directly to observable outcomes.
Search volume for 'cancel Airbnb' spikes 40% in a week—does that correlate with what the human-expressive layer is picking up?
What We Already Do
Brand tracking Journey mapping Market sizing Segmentation Competitive benchmarking Pricing research
Rationale
Your CMO doesn't care what people say about the brand in isolation. They care whether the gap between perception and behavior is widening or closing.
Layer 03
Cultural & Contextual
The 'why is this happening' layer. Contextualizes the other two layers.
When 15 tech journalists all shift from writing 'Airbnb disrupts hospitality' to 'Airbnb's regulatory reckoning,' that's a frame shift.
What We Already Do
Social analytics Online anthropology Semiotics Cultural strategy Trend forecasting
Rationale
Social listening, news corpus analysis, Reddit/forum threading, analyst commentary, and competitor ad creative monitoring.

Design Architecture

Architected Spectrum of Design Complexity

5 UI philosophy tiers. Pick based on your team's sophistication and use case.
Tier 1
One Number, One Action
Reference: Robinhood / Cash App
Radical simplification. Brand health score trending up or down, with a single-sentence explanation.
Best Fit: Synthetic tier, non-technical marketing leaders
Tier 2
Guided Workflow with Depth
Reference: TurboTax / Notion
Conversational interface surfacing the most significant signals with confidence indicators.
Best Fit: Middle tier, clients with data literacy but not dedicated analysts
Tier 3
Opinionated Canvas
Reference: Linear / Figma
Workspace model with multi-panel canvas. Keyboard shortcuts for power users.
Best Fit: Survey-Anchored, dedicated internal analytics teams
Tier 4
Modular Intelligence Panels
Reference: Stripe / Datadog
Full dashboard architecture with configurable widgets. Alert systems for threshold crossings.
Best Fit: Large enterprise, multiple teams touch the data
Tier 5
Raw Signal Access with Models
Reference: Bloomberg Terminal
The power-user endpoint. Raw embeddings visible, queryable, and exportable.
Best Fit: Internal Material+ tool and enterprise sales demo

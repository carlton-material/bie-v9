Three Signal Types

Multiple Imperfect Signals > One Clean Source

The system surfaces agreement and conflict, allowing you to see where signals converge and where they diverge.

Human-Expressive
Human Voice
AI-moderated interviews, open-ended prompts, brand narratives, and consumer storytelling. The emotional layer.
Behavioral & Market
Market Signals
Sales data, search behavior, web engagement, pipeline velocity, and purchase intent. The economic reality.
Cultural & Contextual
Narrative Layer
News narratives, competitor messaging, social signals, and cultural drift. The macro context.
Multiple imperfect signals are more robust than one authoritative source. Diversity of signal types creates resilience.

Applied Architecture in Motion

Working prototype connecting consumer segments → demand spaces → market share → strategic recommendations. Suntory case demonstrates the viability of the system on a real problem with real stakes.

"I want McKinsey in my pocket."
Jing, VP Insights & Innovation, Beam Suntory

The prototype uses Stayworthy (fictional hotel-tech brand) as the anchor. The system maps consumer behavior → travel segments → hotel selection logic → competitive positioning → what-if scenarios. All in 72 hours.

Live prototype with Stayworthy dataset · Consumer surveys (N=840M behavioral signals) · 3-day build cycle · $0 customer research cost
Three-Tier Delivery Model

Anchor Shrinks. System Grows.

Three pricing tiers that start with primary research and systematically reduce the anchor as the AI system learns and matures.

1
Fully Synthetic
LLM-generated audiences. No primary research. Pure signal synthesis across behavioral, cultural, and synthetic human interviews.
3-day turnaround
Lowest cost
2
Historical + Augmented
Leverage existing client data (surveys, CRM, transaction history). Layer new signals on top. Richer output. Faster than primary research.
Moderate cost
5-7 day turnaround
3
Survey-Anchored
Lightweight primary research (online). All three signal types. Highest confidence. Premium output for strategic decisions.
Premium tier
10-14 day turnaround

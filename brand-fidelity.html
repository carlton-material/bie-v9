What is M+ Brand Fidelity?

The proprietary framework that predicts business outcomes from brand relationships

6 drivers. 2 dimensions. One score that matters.

Methodology

The Framework

Brand Fidelity measures how deeply and consistently a brand relationship drives business outcomes. It synthesizes six evidence-based drivers into a single composite score that predicts advocacy, profitability, and competitive differentiation.

The Research

Study Design
Material+ Brand Fidelity is grounded in rigorous research across 24,000 consumers, 229 brands, and 30 categories. The framework was validated using Forrester methodology and tested against transactional outcomes across hospitality, retail, financial services, and technology sectors.

Scoring

The Composite Score

The Brand Fidelity Composite is calculated as an unweighted average of the six drivers. This approach treats each dimension equally because each has independent predictive power across different business outcomes.

Composite = (User Friendly + Personal + Accessible + Dependable + Meaningful + Salient) ÷ 6

The result is a single, trackable number that reflects overall brand relationship health and predicts customer behavior change 4-8 weeks in advance.
